About

About Me

I am a PhD researcher in Transportation Engineering at the University of Mississippi (Ole Miss), working as a Graduate Research and Teaching Assistant under Dr. Amir M. Molan. My primary research focuses on NCHRP Project 17-131 — developing a national practitioner guide for Alternative Intersections and Interchanges (AII), including safety surrogate frameworks, operational analysis, and design guidance for unconventional configurations such as RCUTs, MUTs, and DDIs.

I hold a Master's in Transportation Engineering from Utah State University (GPA: 3.71) and bring hands-on experience across two countries — from UDOT-funded VRU safety research in Utah to roadway design projects in Nepal. I am passionate about evidence-based roadway safety, GIS-driven crash analysis, and translating research into actionable DOT guidance.
